Company profile

We, Kamal E Industries, are one of the prime Manufacturers, Exporters and Suppliers of Machinery used in processing and packaging of products from domains like food, beverage, pharmaceutical, chemical and more. Our wide range of products include Planetary Mixer, Capsule Filling Machine, Bottle Capping Machine, Pulverisor, Bung Washing Machine, Conveyors, Tableting Machine, etc. We are supported by a sound manufacturing unit; we have been offering quality solutions that are reckoned for high speed and performance. Our team of experts allows us to design unbeatable products in bulk to match up with the requisites of the clients. We have been offering the product at reasonable prices within the stipulated time period so that our clients do not face any kind of problem.
